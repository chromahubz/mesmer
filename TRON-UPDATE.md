## ğŸ¨ TRON SHADER FEATURES:

### Neon Grid:
Classic TRON floor grid with perspective depth. Cyan and magenta neon lines that pulse with the music. Perfect for that 1982 aesthetic!

### Wireframe Tunnel:
Rotating tunnel made of wireframe rings and spokes. Audio-reactive rotation speed. Cyan to orange gradient colors.

### Vector Lines:
Multiple glowing vector lines rotating around the center. Each line has its own color gradient. Super smooth and hypnotic.

### Circuit Board:
Realistic PCB (circuit board) patterns with traces and component pads. Pads pulse with the beat. Green and blue neon traces.

### Scan Lines:
CRT monitor simulation with horizontal scan lines, RGB separation, and digital glitches. Includes vignette effect and sync issues that glitch with audio.

### Light Cycles:
Multiple colored light trails moving in circular paths. Each trail glows and leaves a luminous path behind. Pure TRON energy!

### Hexagon Grid:
Honeycomb pattern with neon-edged hexagons. Each hex pulses independently. Creates a futuristic beehive effect.

### Digital Rain:
Matrix-style falling characters/code. Green phosphor glow on black. Head of each column glows brightest and responds to audio.

### Vector Scope:
Lissajous patterns like on an oscilloscope. Multiple waveforms with different frequencies. Classic vector graphics look.

### Retro Blocks:
Early CGI-style blocks that scale and pulse. Cyan, magenta, and yellow colors. Gives that 1980s computer graphics vibe.
